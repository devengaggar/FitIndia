About FitIndia.pro

Indian-first Evidence-based AI Calorie Tracker Clinician-guided

FitIndia.pro is India’s first truly practical, evidence-based health companion—one built for your plate, your pace, and your busy routine.

We believe staying healthy shouldn't demand lifestyle upheaval. So instead of calorie-counting by Western standards, we bring health strategies rooted in Indian daily life:

  • Everyday Indian meals, not fancy grocery hauls—think roti–sabzi, dal–chawal, idli, and one-pot Indian meals.
  • Guidance from clinical nutrition experts, not influencers—professionally designed plans that incorporate BMI, Waist-Hip Ratios, step goals, and mental wellbeing.
  • No overwhelm: day-wise plans with weekly goals, gentle WhatsApp nudges, and a free AI calorie tracker that works with your meal photos or descriptions.
  • Affordable & accessible: no hidden calculators, no premium lockdowns. Light, mobile-friendly design for tier-2 & tier-3 users, too.
  • Community-powered motivation: local 7- and 30-day challenges, and India’s first obesity-focused healthcare ecosystem—expanding with clinics, pharma, and fitness partners.

Whether you're juggling work, family, and meals—FitIndia.pro makes health doable, Indian, and sustainable.

Our Vision

To make India healthier, one person at a time, by becoming the country’s most trusted digital ecosystem for obesity prevention, treatment, and long-term weight management. We aim to bridge the gap between healthcare expertise and everyday lifestyle—so that anyone, anywhere in India, can access the right tools, care, and community to achieve lasting wellness.

Our Mission

  • Empower 10,000+ Indians by 2025 with sustainable, evidence-based obesity care.
  • Combine medical expertise with technology, making weight management simple, personalized, and accessible.
  • Partner with doctors, nutritionists, and healthcare leaders, creating India’s first integrated obesity-care platform.
  • Foster a supportive community through challenges, education, and motivation—helping individuals stay consistent and inspired.
  • Set global benchmarks in digital obesity management rooted in Indian lifestyle and culture.
